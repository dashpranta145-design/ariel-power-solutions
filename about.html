Who We Are

Your trusted partner in renewable energy engineering

Ariel Power Solutions is a smart renewable energy engineering company committed to powering Bangladesh's transition toward a sustainable and energy-secure future. We design, install, and maintain residential, commercial, industrial, and utility-scale solar power systems, providing fully integrated turnkey solutions tailored to each client's needs.

Backed by an expert team of engineers and energy professionals, we combine innovation, efficiency, and reliability to deliver world-class solar projects that perform — and last. Our proven track record spans both government and private sectors, including institutions such as PWD, HED, EED, as well as industries, garments factories, banks, and corporate facilities nationwide.

At Ariel Power Solutions, we believe clean energy is not just an alternative — it is the foundation of progress. Our purpose is to reduce the nation's electricity deficit, accelerate the Government's renewable energy goals, and ensure safe, affordable, and sustainable power for every corner of Bangladesh.

Our Vision & Mission

Driving Bangladesh toward a greener, cleaner, and more resilient energy future

Our Vision

To become Bangladesh's most trusted and innovative solar energy partner — driving the nation's transformation toward a greener, cleaner, and more resilient energy future.

We envision a country where every home, business, and industry is powered by renewable energy — minimizing dependence on fossil fuels, reducing carbon emissions, and protecting the environment for generations to come.

Ariel Power Solutions strives to lead this transformation with technical excellence, integrity, and sustainability at the core of everything we do.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver dependable, efficient, and high-performance solar energy solutions that empower people, businesses, and institutions to embrace renewable power with confidence.

We aim to support the Government of Bangladesh's clean energy objectives by bridging the electricity gap, ensuring energy security, and contributing to a low-carbon economy.

Through expert engineering, innovative technology, and uncompromising quality, Ariel Power Solutions is building a brighter, more sustainable future — one solar system at a time.
