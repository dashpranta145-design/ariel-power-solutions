Understanding Your Investment

Transparent breakdown of capital and operational expenditures

CapEx

Capital Expenditure

Initial project investment including solar panels, inverters, structures, installation, and commissioning. We optimize CapEx to ensure cost-effectiveness without compromising quality or performance.

  • Solar Panels: Tier-1 high-efficiency modules with 25+ year warranty
  • Inverters: Premium quality string or hybrid inverters
  • Mounting Structures: Certified, weather-resistant aluminum structures
  • Installation: Professional EPC services with safety compliance
  • Commissioning: Complete testing and grid integration

OpEx

Operational Expenditure

Covers ongoing operation, maintenance, monitoring, and servicing throughout the project life cycle. Designed to keep your system performing at its best — with predictable, low operating costs.

  • Preventive Maintenance: Regular cleaning and inspection services
  • Real-time Monitoring: 24/7 performance tracking and analytics
  • Troubleshooting: Rapid response to faults and issues
  • Component Servicing: Inverter and electrical system maintenance
  • Performance Optimization: Continuous efficiency improvements
