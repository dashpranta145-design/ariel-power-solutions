Residential Rooftop Solar (On-Grid / Hybrid)

Power your home with clean, affordable solar energy. Our Residential Solar Systems are designed for long-term performance, energy savings, and a sustainable lifestyle.

On-Grid System: Connects to the national grid through Net Metering, reducing your monthly electricity cost.

Hybrid System: Includes smart battery backup for uninterrupted power during grid outages.

Each system is custom-engineered using Tier-1 solar panels, high-efficiency inverters, and certified mounting structures. We handle everything — survey, design, approval, installation, and maintenance — ensuring a smooth experience for every homeowner.

Government Subsidies, Grants & Tax Incentives

We assist clients in accessing government-backed rebates, grants, and tax exemptions available under Bangladesh's renewable energy policies. These incentives help improve project feasibility and shorten the payback period.

Government Subsidies

Access financial support from renewable energy programs designed to reduce upfront installation costs and make solar energy more affordable for homeowners and businesses across Bangladesh.

Grant Programs

Benefit from national and international grant opportunities for solar projects. We guide you through the application process to secure funding that accelerates your transition to renewable energy.

Tax Exemptions

Take advantage of VAT exemptions, duty-free imports on solar equipment, and corporate tax holidays available under Bangladesh's green energy initiatives, maximizing your investment returns.

Net Metering Benefits

Sell excess solar power back to the grid through Bangladesh's Net Metering policy. We handle all regulatory approvals and documentation to ensure seamless grid integration.

Accelerated Depreciation

Leverage accelerated depreciation benefits for commercial and industrial solar installations, allowing faster cost recovery and improved cash flow for your business operations.

Expert Consultation

Our dedicated team provides comprehensive support throughout the incentive application process, ensuring you receive all eligible benefits and maximize your project's financial viability.
